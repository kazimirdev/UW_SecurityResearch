# 01 — Goal and preparation

- The objective was to expose a public VPS accessed by IPv4 as a full-port TCP/UDP honeypot.
- Environment: Debian 13, 1 GB RAM, 1 vCPU, full public internet exposure.
- Logging used `nftables`, `systemd`, honeypot backend, and realtime CSV pipelines.
- Core configs are stored in `vps/etc/` and `vps/opt/`.
