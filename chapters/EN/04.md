# 04 — Conclusions

- TCP was dominated by SSH, Telnet, Web, DB, and administrative services.
- UDP was dominated by mDNS, LLMNR, discovery, and amplification patterns.
- Traffic included both botnet-style brute force and reconnaissance activity.
- Full-port exposure revealed both standard and non-standard scanning strategies.
